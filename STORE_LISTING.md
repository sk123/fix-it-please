# Fix It Please !! — Store Listing (All Languages)

## 🇺🇸 English (en-US)

**Short description (80 chars):**
Request repairs from your landlord instantly via text or email.

**Full description:**
Fix it Please !! makes it easy to request repairs from your landlord or property manager — no typing required.

How it works:

1. Tap the type of problem (plumbing, electrical, appliance, etc.)
2. Tap the room location
3. Choose urgency level
4. Snap a photo if needed
5. Hit Send — opens your text or email app with everything pre-filled

Features:
• Tap-only repair requests — no typing needed
• Send via text message or email
• Request history with status tracking (Pending → In Progress → Resolved)
• Document Vault — store your lease, walkthrough photos, receipts, and correspondence
• Voice notes — record notes by speaking or typing
• Landlord contact auto-save — enter once, filled in automatically next time
• 100% private — all data stays on your phone, nothing is uploaded to anyone.

Built for tenants who want a simple, organized way to keep track of everything related to their tenancy.

💡 Have an idea for a feature? We'd love to hear it — this app is just getting started and there's much more to come. Reach out anytime!
