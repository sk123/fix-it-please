Privacy Policy

Fix it Please
Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

Fix it Please is a mobile application that helps tenants report maintenance issues to their landlord or property manager. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All information you enter into the app — including repair requests, landlord contact details, notes, and uploaded documents — is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. No data is ever sent to our servers or any third party.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear your device's storage, this data will be permanently deleted. We have no ability to access, recover, or view your data.

Third-Party Services

Fix it Please does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data processing services.

Communications

When you use the "Text" or "Email" feature, the app opens your device's native SMS or email application with a pre-filled message. The actual sending of the message is handled entirely by your device's messaging app, not by Fix it Please.

Photos and Files

Photos taken or files uploaded within the app are stored only on your device. They are not uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

Fix it Please is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
